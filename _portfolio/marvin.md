Under the guidance of Dr. Mili Shah, I am developing robotic arms designed for direct human-robot interaction. Using MediaPipe and OpenCV for pose landmark detection, I implemented real-time forward kinematic mapping from human pose landmarks to the joints of the OpenMANIPULATOR-X system within the ROS2 publisher and subscriber ecosystem. Additionally, we have used Gazebo and MoveIt2 to simulate real-time robotic systems, ensuring collision-free motion planning. We are now working to integrate compliant control and a demo website this semester. 

The project is open-source and can be found on [GitHub](https://github.com/jaehho/Marvin).
