**About Jaeho Cho**

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Jaeho Cho, an aspiring Electrical Engineer currently studying at The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art in New York City, where I'm honing my skills and fueling my passion for innovation. With a projected graduation date in 2026, I'm on a journey fueled by curiosity and the drive to make a tangible impact in the world of technology and bioengineering.

**Education and Scholarships**  
My academic journey is distinguished by a Half Tuition Scholarship & Myron Coe Scholarship, acknowledging my academic achievements and potential. I've immersed myself in a variety of courses, from Electronics and Signal Processing to Computer Architecture and Biology, laying a robust foundation for my career.

**Experience**  
I've had the privilege of contributing to a Vertically Integrated Project on Bioengineering at The Cooper Union, focusing on developing an open-source robotic solution for real-time motion tracking. My role in designing a PCB for an eTextile for the ACM student research competition has been a highlight of my practical learning experience. Beyond the lab, I've shared my knowledge as a Teacher Assistant in Design and Drawing, teaching high school students the fundamentals of engineering design and leading workshops on Arduinos and circuit design.

**Projects**  
My projects range from developing a prosthetic hand with an original actuation system to an electronic glove assisting hand movement, showcasing my commitment to creating solutions that enhance lives. Additionally, I'm exploring the gaming world by developing an original boxing game, demonstrating my versatility and creative thinking.

**Leadership and Community Engagement**  
Leadership is at the core of my journey. As the co-founder of BAG (Beyond A Goal), a non-profit organization, I've led efforts to provide basic hygiene products to the homeless community, raising over $11,000 and donating over 400 bags. My role as the president of the Robotics Team and Physics & Chemistry Clubs in high school further exemplifies my leadership skills and my commitment to fostering a love for STEM.

**Skills**  
I'm proficient in programming languages like Python, Matlab, C, C++, and Git, among others, and skilled in technologies such as ROS2, Blender, and Unity. I'm fluent in English, Korean, and Spanish, enabling me to collaborate effectively in diverse teams.
